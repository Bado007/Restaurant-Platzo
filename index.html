About

Platzo, a venue in the heart of Stockholm.

We offer a culinary experience and festive environment above the ordinary. Here you can find traditional lunch and dinner but also clubs and exciting events all year around.

We are a dynamic tavern that serve various types of dishes, both classic and modern fusion cuisine. Our focus lies as much on the service as on the atmosphere, and always on our guests. Welcome in for a treat.
